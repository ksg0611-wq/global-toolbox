Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: How long should a cold email pitch to a brand be?

A: Keep your cold email concise, ideally under 150-200 words. Brand managers and marketing agencies are busy and receive hundreds of pitches. A short pitch with a clear value proposition is much more likely to be read fully and answered.

Q: What makes a subject line successful for brand sponsorships?

A: A successful subject line is professional, clear, and co-branded. Avoid vague lines like 'Sponsorship opportunity' or 'Partnership request'. Instead, use structured formats like 'Partnership: [Your Name] x [Brand Name]' or 'Content Collaboration - [Your Name] ([Subscribers] Subs)'.

Q: Should I include my rates in the initial pitch email?

A: Generally, no. The goal of the initial email is to start a conversation and gauge their interest. Once they reply expressing interest, you can send your media kit and discuss custom packages and pricing based on their specific campaign objectives.
